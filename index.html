Hydrogeology

Geotechnical Engineering

  • All aspects of geotechnical site characterisation, mapping, trial pits, geotechnical boreholes, CPTu Tests, geophysical surveys, in situ materials testing and specialised sampling, and contractor procurement & supervision.

  • Geotechnical Design.
  • Foundation design, lateral support & retaining walls design, slope stability assessment, de-watering and drainage assessment, and coastal defence design.

  • Construction.
  • Contractors procurement & supervision, and construction quality control & monitoring.

About

  • Baleta Earth Science
